Admission Requirements

Freshmen

  • Report Card (DepEd Form 138) or its equivalent (F-137) and NCAE Results
  • Physical and medical examination certified by a government physician, certifying the applicant’s fitness to do University work.
  • Certificate of good moral character from the school last attended.
  • Three (3) latest 2 x 2 pictures
  • Photocopy of authenticated birth certificate issued by NSO

Student Transferees

  • Transfer Credential/Honorable Dismissal from the school last attended.
  • Transcript of Records or a copy of Academic Records for evaluation purposes.
  • Physical and medical examination certified by a government physician certifying the applicant’s fitness to do University work.
  • Certificate of good moral character from the school last attended.
  • Three (3) latest 2 x 2 pictures.
  • Photocopy of authenticated birth certificate issued by NSO.

Applicants (New Freshmen and Student Transferees) must pass the entrance examination and interview.

Transfer Admission

Transfer within LSPU/SHIFTING of COURSE

A student who has earned at least 30 collegiate academic units and wishes to transfer from one course to another should file in his current college an application for transfer/shifting of course.

His application is referred to the accepting College together with his true copy of grades. If the action of the accepting college is favorable, the student gets his permit to transfer and have copy of grades.

Shifting of courses is not allowed unless for valid reason/s. Those who intend to transfer should have qualified for the grade requirements of the University /department where the student will transfer. Application form for shifting of course is made on the prescribed form indicating the reason/s for shifting with the attached copy of grades duly certified by the registrar. The request for shifting courses must be approved by the College Dean to be submitted to the VPAA/Campus Director for his approval before 12% of the regular class meeting has been held during the term.

Any request submitted and or received thereafter shall not be considered for the following semester.

TRANSFER within LSPU SYSTEM

Students from another constituent campus who have earned at least 30 collegiate academic units may be admitted as transfer students subject to the rules of the admitting College.

Advanced Credits

Any student whose application for admission has already been approved may apply for advanced credits for work done in another college /university upon presentation of credentials showing the subjects attended and passed in the said institution, full equivalent to those given in this University for which advanced credit is sought and upon passing or complying with the requirements for validation.
